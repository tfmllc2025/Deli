# /competitor-check — See What Other Delis Are Doing

Read `marketing/config/business-profile.json` first.

**What this does:** Looks at other delis and convenience stores near Bellport to see what they're doing well, what they're doing badly, and where Quickstop Super Deli has an advantage.

## Important: How to Write Your Output

The person using this is not a tech or marketing person. Follow these rules:
- **Plain language.** Don't say "competitive positioning." Say "here's what makes your deli better."
- **Keep it practical.** For every finding, explain what to actually *do* about it.
- **Use a simple format.** For each competitor: name, where they are, their Google rating, and 1-2 bullet points about what they do well or badly.
- **End with a clear "here's what to do" section.** 3 simple action items max.

## What to Research

### 1. Nearby Competitors
Search for delis, bagel shops, and convenience stores in:
- Bellport
- East Patchogue
- Patchogue
- Medford
- Brookhaven (hamlet)

For each one, keep it simple:
- **Name** and where they are
- **Google rating** and how many reviews they have
- **Hours** — do they open as early as 5 AM?
- **Delivery** — are they on DoorDash or GrubHub?
- **One thing they do well** (based on their reviews)
- **One thing customers complain about**

### 2. Where Quickstop Wins
Based on the competitors, point out clear advantages:
- Opens earlier than them?
- Closer to the LIRR station?
- On more delivery apps?
- Offers something they don't?
- Has better reviews?

### 3. Google Reviews Comparison
- How does Quickstop's rating compare to the top 5 nearby spots?
- How many reviews does each have?
- What do people love about the competitors? (So we can match it)
- What do people hate about the competitors? (So we can avoid it or capitalize)

### 4. Are They on Social Media?
Quick check — do the nearby competitors post on Instagram or Facebook?
- If they don't: that's an advantage for Quickstop
- If they do: how often do they post? Is it good?

### 5. What to Do About It (3 Actions Max)
Based on everything above, give exactly 3 things to do:
1. One thing to start doing (that competitors aren't)
2. One thing to emphasize more (an existing advantage)
3. One thing to learn from a competitor (something they do well that Quickstop should try)

Keep each action to 2-3 sentences. No big strategy docs.

## Output
Save to `marketing/output/` with filename like `competitor-check-YYYY-MM-DD.md`.
