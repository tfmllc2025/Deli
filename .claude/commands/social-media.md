# /social-media — Create Social Media Posts for the Week

Read `marketing/config/business-profile.json`, `marketing/config/brand-voice.md`, and `marketing/config/target-audiences.md` first.

**What this does:** Creates ready-to-post Instagram and Facebook content for the deli — captions you can copy and paste, hashtags included, plus simple directions for what photos to take.

## Important: How to Write Your Output

The person using this is not a tech or marketing person. Follow these rules:
- **Copy-paste ready.** Every caption and hashtag set should be ready to paste directly into Instagram or Facebook. No placeholders.
- **Simple photo directions.** Don't say "capture the hero shot with natural backlighting." Say "Take a photo of the sandwich from above on the counter. Use the window light if you can."
- **Include posting instructions.** For each post, briefly say how to post it (e.g., "Open Instagram → tap the + at the bottom → pick the photo → paste the caption below → tap Share").
- **No marketing lingo.** Say "post" not "content." Say "theme" not "content pillar." Say "the caption" not "the copy."
- **Keep it manageable.** 5 posts for the week is plenty. Don't overwhelm.

## What to Generate

### Instagram / Facebook Posts (5 for the week)
For each post, provide:
- **What day to post it** and roughly what time
- **Where to post it** (Instagram, Facebook, or both)
- **The caption** (ready to copy-paste)
- **Hashtags** (ready to copy-paste as a separate block — 10-15 hashtags)
- **What photo to take** (simple, specific directions — what food, what angle, what background)
- **Theme** (which of the 5 themes below it falls under)

### Themes to Rotate Through
1. **The Food** — Photos of sandwiches, chicken over rice, fresh coffee
2. **Morning Rush** — Early morning energy, LIRR commuters, "5 AM and we're rolling"
3. **Convenience Store** — Store items, snacks, essentials ("forgot milk? We got you")
4. **Bellport Community** — Local shoutouts, weather posts, neighborhood vibes
5. **Behind the Counter** — Making food, the team, real deli life

### Story / Reel Ideas (2 simple ones)
For each, provide:
- What to film (keep it simple — phone camera is fine)
- How long it should be (15-30 seconds max)
- What text to put on screen
- Step-by-step: how to post it as a Story or Reel

## Voice Reminders
- Casual, warm, like a guy behind the counter who knows your order
- "Hero" not "sub", "BEC" not "bacon egg and cheese sandwich", "on line" not "in line"
- Occasionally funny, never forced
- No corporate speak, no influencer tone
