# /weekly-plan — This Week's Marketing Plan

Read `marketing/config/business-profile.json`, `marketing/config/brand-voice.md`, and `marketing/config/target-audiences.md` first. Also check `marketing/content/calendar/content-calendar.md` for any existing plans.

**What this does:** Creates a simple day-by-day plan for the deli's marketing this week. Open it Monday morning and follow it — every post, photo, and task is spelled out.

## Important: How to Write Your Output

The person using this is not a tech or marketing person. Follow these rules:
- **Day-by-day format.** Monday through Friday (or Sunday if relevant). One section per day.
- **Each day should take 10-15 minutes max.** If a day's tasks take longer, you've made it too complicated.
- **Every caption is copy-paste ready.** No placeholders, no "[insert photo here]." Just the text to paste.
- **Photo directions are specific and simple.** "Take a photo of a BEC on deli paper, from above" not "capture an appetizing flat lay."
- **Include posting steps every time.** Don't assume he remembers from last week. Brief is fine: "Open Instagram → tap + → pick photo → paste caption → Share."
- **Lead each day with what matters most.** If there's only one thing to do that day, say so.
- **Use plain language.** Say "Google listing" not "GBP." Say "post" not "content."

## What to Output

### For Each Day of the Week

**[Day] — [One-sentence summary of what to do today]**

1. **Post to make today:**
   - Where to post (Instagram, Facebook, or both)
   - The caption (copy-paste ready)
   - The hashtags (copy-paste ready, separate block)
   - What photo to take (simple, specific)
   - How to post it (brief steps)

2. **Quick task (if any):**
   - One small thing that takes under 5 minutes (reply to a review, check a delivery listing, etc.)
   - Step-by-step instructions

### Plus These Once for the Week

**Google Listing Post (do this on Monday):**
- The post text (copy-paste ready)
- How to post it: Open the Google Business Profile app → tap "Add update" → paste → add photo → Post

**This Week's Special:**
- The deal (one sentence)
- Sign text (what to write/print for the counter)
- Instagram caption to announce it

**Photos to Take This Week:**
- A simple list of 5 specific photos to take whenever there's a quiet moment
- Each one says: what food/scene, what angle, where to stand
- Say which day's post each photo is for

## Format
Output as a clean markdown file organized by day. Save to `marketing/content/calendar/` with the week's date range as the filename (e.g., `week-2026-02-16.md`).
