# /promotions — Promotion & Special Ideas

Read `marketing/config/business-profile.json`, `marketing/config/brand-voice.md`, and `marketing/config/target-audiences.md` first.

**What this does:** Comes up with deals, specials, and promotion ideas for the deli — things that bring in more customers or get regulars to come back more often. Everything is designed for a family-run shop with no marketing budget.

## Important: How to Write Your Output

The person using this is not a tech or marketing person. Follow these rules:
- **Keep it simple.** Each idea should be explainable in 2-3 sentences.
- **Ready to go.** Include the exact sign text, the social media caption, and what to tell customers.
- **No marketing jargon.** Say "deal" or "special" not "promotion campaign." Say "regulars" not "customer segment."
- **Be realistic.** This is a deli, not a chain. No ideas that need an app, a website update, or a graphic designer. Handwritten signs, printed cards, and word-of-mouth are the tools.
- **One great idea > five okay ideas.** Lead with the best one.

## What to Generate

Based on the current date and season, produce:

### 1. This Week's Special (The Main Idea)
- **Name it** — something catchy and simple
- **The deal** — explain it in one sentence
- **What to write on the sign** — exact text for a counter sign or window sign (keep it short, big letters)
- **What to post on Instagram/Facebook** — the caption, ready to paste
- **Who it's for** — commuters? regulars? delivery customers? One sentence on why this deal works for them.

### 2. Monthly Theme
- What's happening this month (holidays, weather, local events, sports)?
- One simple theme to tie the month together
- 2-3 things to do this month (keep each one to 1-2 sentences)

### 3. Loyalty Idea
- A simple "come back" idea (punch cards, "5th coffee free," etc.)
- Keep it low-tech: paper punch cards you can buy on Amazon, or a simple stamp system
- Include what to write on the card and how to explain it to customers

### 4. Delivery App Ideas
- One idea for DoorDash and one for GrubHub
- Things like: bundle deals, free delivery hours, featured item
- Include step-by-step instructions for how to set up the promotion on each app (which menu to open in the merchant dashboard, what to click)

### 5. Weather Special
Based on this week's weather for Bellport, NY:
- Cold/rainy → warm food and drink deals
- Hot/sunny → cold sandwich and iced drink deals
- Include the sign text and Instagram caption
