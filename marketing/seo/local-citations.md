## How to Claim a Listing

Most of these work the same way:
1. Go to the website listed above
2. Search for "Quickstop Super Deli" or the address
3. If it's already there, look for a "Claim this business" button
4. If it's not there, look for "Add a business" button
5. Fill in the info (use the exact name, address, and phone above)
6. They'll verify you own the business — usually by phone call, postcard, or email

## Important Rule

The name, address, and phone number must be **exactly the same** on every listing. Don't use "Quick Stop" on one and "Quickstop" on another. Don't use "Station Road" on one and "Station Rd" on another. Keep it identical everywhere.
