# Google Business Profile — Setup & Optimization

The Google listing is the single most important thing for a local deli online. When someone searches "deli near me" on their phone, this is what shows up. It needs to be complete and accurate.

## What Is This?

When you search for a business on Google or Google Maps, you see a box with the name, address, hours, photos, and reviews. That's the Google Business Profile (the "Google listing"). You can control what it says.

## How to Update Your Google Listing

1. Download the **Google Business Profile** app on your phone (or go to business.google.com on a computer)
2. Sign in with the Google account that owns the listing
3. You'll see your business dashboard
4. Tap **Edit profile** to update info (hours, phone, description, etc.)
5. Tap **Add photo** to upload new food photos
6. Tap **Add update** to write a post (like a mini social media post on Google)

## Weekly Task: Post to Google

Every Monday, post a short update to the Google listing. This helps you show up higher in search results. Use the `/local-seo` command to generate these posts.

**How to post:**
1. Open the Google Business Profile app
2. Tap **Add update**
3. Choose **Add update** (not offer or event)
4. Paste your post text
5. Add a photo
6. Tap **Post**
