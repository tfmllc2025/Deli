# Example Facebook Posts — Quickstop Super Deli

Facebook posts can be a bit longer than Instagram and don't need hashtags. Use these as style examples.

---

## Example 1: General / Food

**Photo:** A nice spread — a couple sandwiches, a coffee, maybe some chips. On the counter.

**Caption:**
```
Lunch is ready at Quickstop Super Deli.

Cold cut heroes, hot sandwiches, chicken over rice, wraps — whatever you're in the mood for. Made fresh, ready fast.

Stop by 461 Station Rd in Bellport or order delivery on DoorDash and GrubHub.
```

---

## Example 2: Weather / Community

**Photo:** The storefront, especially if it's snowy or rainy outside.

**Caption:**
```
Cold and rainy out there today, Bellport. But we're open.

Stay warm out there.
```

## How to Post on Facebook

1. Open **Facebook** on your phone or computer
2. Go to your **business page** (not your personal profile)
3. Tap **Create post** (or "What's on your mind?" at the top)
4. Paste the caption from above
5. Tap the **photo icon** to add a picture
6. Pick a photo from your phone
7. Tap **Post**
