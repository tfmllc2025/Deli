## How to Post on Instagram

1. Open **Instagram** on your phone
2. Tap the **+** button at the bottom of the screen
3. Choose **Post**
4. Take a photo or pick one from your camera roll
5. Tap **Next** (you can skip filters — real photos look best)
6. Tap **Next** again
7. Paste the caption from above into the text box
8. Paste the hashtags at the end (or in a comment right after posting)
9. Tap **Share**
