# Example Google Listing Posts — Quickstop Super Deli

Google listing posts show up when people search for the deli on Google. They help you show up higher in search results. Aim for 1 post per week.

---

## Example 1: General

```
Looking for a great deli near the Bellport LIRR station? Quickstop Super Deli is right across the street at 461 Station Rd. We're open every day at 5 AM with fresh breakfast sandwiches, hot coffee, cold cut heroes, chicken over rice, and more. Plus we carry all the convenience store essentials you need. Stop by before your train or order delivery through DoorDash and GrubHub. We've got you covered, Bellport.
```

## Example 2: Seasonal (Winter)

Grab breakfast before the train or stop by for lunch. Hot sandwiches, chicken over rice, heroes, and everything you need to get through the day. Delivery available on DoorDash and GrubHub.

---

## How to Post on Your Google Listing

1. Open the **Google Business Profile** app on your phone
   - (If you don't have it, search "Google Business Profile" in the App Store or Google Play and download it)
2. Sign in with the Google account connected to the deli's listing
3. Tap **Add update** (or the **+** button)
4. Choose **Add update**
5. Paste the text from above
6. Tap **Add photo** and pick a food photo from your phone
7. Tap **Post**

**Do this every Monday.** It takes 2 minutes and helps people find you on Google.
