# Content Calendar — Quickstop Super Deli

This file tracks the weekly marketing schedule. Use `/weekly-plan` to generate each week's plan, and the generated files will be saved in this folder.

## Weekly Routine

| Day | What to Do | Time Needed |
|---|---|---|
| **Monday** | Post on Google listing + Instagram post | 15 min |
| **Tuesday** | Instagram post | 10 min |
| **Wednesday** | Instagram post + Facebook post | 10 min |
| **Thursday** | Instagram post | 10 min |
| **Friday** | Instagram post + check Google reviews | 15 min |
| **Saturday** | Take photos of food for next week | 15 min |
| **Sunday** | Off (or plan next week's special) | — |

## Monthly Tasks

- [ ] Run `/weekly-plan` for each week
- [ ] Check and reply to all Google reviews
- [ ] Post 1 Google listing update per week (4/month)
- [ ] Check Instagram follower count — is it growing?
- [ ] Check DoorDash and GrubHub ratings
- [ ] Look at what posts did best this month — make more like those

## Generated Weekly Plans

As you generate weekly plans using `/weekly-plan`, they'll be saved in this folder with names like:
- `week-2026-02-16.md`
- `week-2026-02-23.md`
- etc.
