# Template: Daily Special Post

Use this as a starting point whenever you're posting about a daily special.

---

## Counter Sign Text

Keep it big and short — this goes on a sign customers see at the counter or in the window.

```
[SPECIAL NAME]
[What the deal is — one line]
[Today Only / This Week / etc.]
```

**Example:**
```
MONDAY MORNING DEAL
Free Coffee with Any Breakfast Sandwich
Every Monday in February
```

---

## Instagram / Facebook Caption

```
[Catchy opening line about the special]

[One sentence about what the deal is]

[One sentence about why — weather, day of the week, season, etc.]

Stop by or order delivery — link in bio.

[Hashtags on a separate line]
```

**Example:**
```
Monday morning just got better ☕

Free coffee with any breakfast sandwich — today only. Because Mondays are hard enough.

Stop by before the train or order delivery — link in bio.

#BellportNY #BellportDeli #QuickstopSuperDeli #LongIslandEats #BECSandwich #FreeCoffee #MondayMotivation #LIRRCommute
```

---

## How to Post This

**Instagram:**
1. Open Instagram on your phone
2. Tap the **+** button at the bottom center
3. Take a photo of the food (or pick one from your camera roll)
4. Tap **Next**, then **Next** again
5. Paste the caption above into the text box
6. Tap **Share**

**Facebook:**
1. Open Facebook and go to your business page
2. Tap **Create post** at the top
3. Paste the caption
4. Tap **Photo** to add a picture
5. Tap **Post**
