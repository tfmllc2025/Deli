# Template: Review Responses

Ready-to-paste responses for Google reviews. Customize the [bracketed parts] with specifics from the actual review.

---

## Good Reviews (4-5 stars)

Keep it short, personal, and mention something from their review.

**Template 1:**
```
Thanks [name]! Glad you enjoyed the [thing they mentioned]. See you next time.
```

**Template 2:**
```
Appreciate it, [name]. [Thing they mentioned] is one of our favorites too. See you at the counter.
```

**Template 3:**
```
[Name], thanks for the kind words. We're here every morning at 5 AM — see you soon.
```

---

## Bad Reviews (1-2 stars)

Stay calm. Don't argue. Say sorry. Offer to fix it. Give the phone number.

**Template 1:**
```
[Name], sorry about that. That's not how we do things. Give us a call at (631) 286-1491 and we'll make it right.
```

**Template 2:**
```
We appreciate the feedback, [name]. Sorry your experience wasn't great — please call us at (631) 286-1491 so we can fix it. We want to do better.
```

---

## Okay Reviews (3 stars)

Thank them, address the concern briefly, invite them back.

**Template 1:**
```
Thanks for the feedback, [name]. Sorry [thing they mentioned] wasn't up to par. We'd love another chance — stop by anytime.
```

**Template 2:**
```
Appreciate you stopping by, [name]. We hear you on [concern]. Come back and let us make it up to you.
```

---

## How to Reply to a Google Review

1. Open the **Google Maps** app on your phone
2. Search for **Quickstop Super Deli**
3. Tap on the deli listing
4. Scroll down to **Reviews**
5. Find the review you want to reply to
6. Tap **Reply**
7. Paste one of the templates above (change the [bracketed parts])
8. Tap **Post reply**

**Tip:** Try to reply within 1-2 days. Replying to every review (good and bad) shows people you care.
