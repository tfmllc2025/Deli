# Template: Community / Local Event Post

Use this whenever something is happening in Bellport or the surrounding area — local events, weather events, school stuff, holidays, LIRR changes, etc.

---

## When to Use This

- Local event in Bellport (fair, parade, farmers market, etc.)
- Weather event (big snowstorm, heat wave, first day of spring)
- School calendar (first day of school, snow day, summer break)
- LIRR changes (schedule change, delays, new train times)
- Holidays and long weekends (Memorial Day, 4th of July, Labor Day)

---

## Instagram / Facebook Caption

```
[Tie into the local event — show you're part of the community]

[How the deli fits in — are you open? Got specials? Can you help?]

[Call to action — stop by, stay warm, grab food, etc.]

[Hashtags]
```

**Example (Snow Day):**
```
Snow day in Bellport ❄️ Schools are closed but we're not.

Hot coffee, breakfast sandwiches, and everything you need without fighting the roads to the grocery store.

Stay warm out there. We're here til 7.

#BellportNY #SnowDay #QuickstopSuperDeli #LongIslandSnow #BellportDeli #HotCoffee #StillOpen #LongIslandEats
```

**Example (Local Event):**
```
Bellport Village Fair this weekend — stop by the deli before or after.

Cold drinks, sandwiches to go, and everything you need for a day out. We're right on Station Rd, across from the train station.

See you there, Bellport.

#BellportNY #BellportVillage #QuickstopSuperDeli #BellportDeli #LongIslandEats #SuffolkCounty #LocalDeli
```

---

## The Point

These posts aren't about selling — they're about showing the deli is part of Bellport. When people see you showing up for the community, they think of you next time they're hungry.
