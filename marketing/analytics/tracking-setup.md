# What to Track — Simple Marketing Metrics

You don't need to be a data person. Just check these numbers once a week to see if things are working. If the numbers are going up, keep doing what you're doing. If they're not, try something different.

## Weekly Check (Every Monday, 10 Minutes)

### Google Listing
**Where to look:** Open the Google Business Profile app → tap "Performance"

- How many people saw your listing this week?
- How many people clicked to call?
- How many people clicked for directions?
- How many people visited the website?

**What "good" looks like:** These numbers should go up over time. If they're flat, you need more reviews and more posts.

### Instagram
**Where to look:** Open Instagram → go to your profile → tap "Professional dashboard" or "Insights"

- How many followers do you have? (Is it growing?)
- How many people saw your posts this week? (Called "reach")
- Which post got the most likes/comments?

**What "good" looks like:** Steady follower growth. Posts getting more likes than last month. If a post does really well, make more like it.

### Facebook
**Where to look:** Open your Facebook business page → tap "Insights"

- How many people saw your posts?
- Any new page likes?

**What "good" looks like:** People seeing your posts and occasionally liking/commenting.

## Daily Glance (30 Seconds)

### DoorDash
**Where to look:** Open the DoorDash Merchant app

- Any new orders?
- Current rating — is it still above 4.5?
- Any new reviews to respond to?

### GrubHub
**Where to look:** Open the GrubHub for Restaurants app

- Any new orders?
- Current rating
- Any new reviews to respond to?

## Monthly Check (First Monday of Each Month)

Spend 15 minutes looking at the bigger picture:

- Google review count: how many total? How many new this month?
- Google star rating: still 4.5+?
- Instagram followers: how many new this month?
- Best-performing post this month — what was it about?
- Yelp: any new reviews?
- Are DoorDash/GrubHub orders increasing?

## Simple Tracking Sheet

You can track this on paper or a simple note on your phone. Just write down these numbers every Monday:

```
Week of: ___________
Google listing views: _____
Google reviews (total): _____
Google rating: _____
Instagram followers: _____
Best post this week: _____
DoorDash rating: _____
GrubHub rating: _____
```

That's it. Don't overthink it.
