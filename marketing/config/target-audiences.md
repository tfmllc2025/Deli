# Target Audiences — Quickstop Super Deli

Three types of customers. Every post, special, and promotion should be aimed at one of these groups.

## 1. LIRR Commuters

- **Who:** People who take the train to and from NYC every day
- **When they come:** 5-7 AM (before the train) and 5-7 PM (after the train)
- **What they want:** Coffee, BEC, grab-and-go food. Speed is everything — they have a train to catch.
- **How to reach them:** Early morning Instagram posts, "catch it before the train" messaging, loyalty cards for daily coffee buyers
- **What they care about:** Fast, consistent, reliable. Same good coffee every morning.

## 2. Neighborhood Regulars

- **Who:** People who live in Bellport and nearby
- **When they come:** Lunch time and weekends
- **What they want:** Good food, familiar faces, a spot they can count on. Also convenience store runs for basics (milk, bread, drinks).
- **How to reach them:** Community-focused posts, local event tie-ins, "we're your neighbor" messaging
- **What they care about:** Quality, friendliness, being a part of the neighborhood.

## 3. Delivery Customers

- **Who:** People in the surrounding area (Bellport, East Patchogue, Patchogue, Medford) ordering through DoorDash or GrubHub
- **When they order:** Lunch and dinner
- **What they want:** Good menu variety, solid portions, food that travels well. They judge by the app photos and ratings.
- **How to reach them:** Good photos on delivery apps, high ratings, promotions through DoorDash/GrubHub
- **What they care about:** Ratings, photos, portion size, delivery speed.
